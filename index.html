MASKS MATTER

Masks Matter is a student-led non-profit organization dedicated to spreading the belief that everyone, students especially, have the capability to design safe, effective, and innovative masks of their own. This summer, we are hosting a mask designing competition to encourage students to apply their creativity and innovation to helping their community in times of crisis. Please join us in our efforts, because every additional mask helps secure a healthier future.

— THE RULES—

  1. The design must be a finished homemade mask
  2. The material needed to assemble the one mask of your design must not exceed $2.00
  3. Apply on the icon below to sign up and receive emails from the team
  4. If you have any questions, email masksmattersf@gmail.com

— SUBMISSION GUIDELINES—

    Please email your submission to masksmattersf@gmail.com before 11:59 on August 9th, 2020 with the following:
    • Heading of the email: “(FIRST AND LAST NAME), Masks Matter Design Competition”
    • A bullet point list of all materials, along with their quantity, dimensions, and costs
    • A detailed bullet point list of step-by-step instructions on how to create your mask
    • “I, (YOUR FIRST AND LAST NAME), hereby consent to allow Masks Matter to publish my mask design onto their website for the purpose of spreading public health awareness.”
    • OPTIONAL: Alternatively, you can create an instructional video and share with us the youtube link
    • AT LEAST 3 high-resolution pictures of you wearing your design (FRONT, BACK, SIDE)
